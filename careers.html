Careers at H.A.V.E.N. Command

Game Developer Intern

We are seeking a talented Game Developer Intern to assist in the development of our gaming projects. Responsibilities include coding, debugging, and implementing game features, as well as collaborating with the design and art teams to ensure a cohesive gaming experience.

2D/3D Modeling and Topology Intern

Join our modeling and topology team as an Intern and help create stunning visual assets for our games. Responsibilities include modeling and texturing characters, environments, and props, as well as optimizing assets for performance and scalability.

Game Design Intern

We are looking for a creative Game Design Intern to assist in the development of game concepts, mechanics, and levels. Responsibilities include designing and prototyping gameplay features, balancing game systems, and analyzing player feedback to improve overall game experience.

Audio Engineer Intern

Join our audio engineering team as an Intern and assist in creating immersive soundscapes for our games. Responsibilities include recording and editing audio assets, implementing sound effects and music, and collaborating with the design team to enhance player immersion.

Marketing Intern

We are seeking a Marketing Intern to assist in promoting our games and engaging with our community. Responsibilities include creating marketing materials, managing social media channels, analyzing marketing data, and assisting in the development of marketing strategies.
